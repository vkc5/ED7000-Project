Can I become a programmer without a degree?
Yes! Many successful programmers are self-taught or have taken online courses and bootcamps.
Do I need to be good at math to learn programming?
Basic math helps, but most programming relies more on logic and problem-solving than advanced math skills.
Do Programming jobs in Bahrain require certifications?
While not always mandatory, certifications significantly boost your chances of employment and help meet the criteria set by many companies, especially in banking, government, and telecom sectors in Bahrain.
