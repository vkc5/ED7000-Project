üìù Project Overview

The EduExplorer project was designed with the goal of creating a comprehensive and user-friendly website that would centralize valuable information about programming, cybersecurity and networking courses. The primary objective was to provide users with easy access to a variety of data, including detailed educational info, rating functionality, filtration system, and other key useful features. By consolidating this data in one place, the project aimed to simplify the process of researching educational info for prospective students. To achieve this, the team worked collaboratively on all aspects of the project, from the initial design phase to the final development. Each team member took on a specific role based on their strengths and expertise, contributing to different parts of the project. Some focused on researching and gathering the necessary data about programming and cybersecurity courses, ensuring that the information was accurate and up to date. Others worked on designing the user interface, striving to create an intuitive and visually appealing experience for users. Additionally, team members with expertise in coding and development contributed to building the website's functionality, focusing on front-end development to ensure the site was both responsive and easy to navigate. Throughout the project, the team coordinated their efforts to ensure that every aspect of the website was aligned with the overarching goal of providing users with an efficient and informative tool for exploring educational info.
